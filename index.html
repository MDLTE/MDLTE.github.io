01 — about

I'm a Robotics & Digital Systems engineer graduating from Tecnológico de Monterrey in June 2026. My work lives at the intersection of hardware and software — building systems that run on real robots, not just in simulation.

I spent six months at AIST in Tokyo, Japan's National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology, developing a teleoperation module for a humanoid robot.
